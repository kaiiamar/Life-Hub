Gua Sha Guide
Drain · Depuff · Glow
Gua sha stimulates lymphatic drainage, reduces puffiness and boosts circulation — giving you that natural glow. Do this 3–4× per week in your evening routine, after toner and oil but before serums. Never drag on dry skin.
Step 1 — Prep Your Skin
Step 2 — The Moves
Step 3 — Continue Routine
Apply serums and moisturiser as normal: Simple HA → Topicals Faded → NMF Moisturiser → Dr. Organic oil to seal. Your skin will absorb everything beautifully after gua sha.
Golden Rules
✦ Never use on dry skin — always have slip from oil or serum
✦ Light pressure is enough — you're draining lymph, not scraping
✦ Each stroke should be slow and deliberate, not rushed
✦ Clean your gua sha after every use with soap and water
✦ Some redness after is normal — it fades within minutes
✦ Never use over active breakouts or broken skin
✦ Results build over weeks — consistency is everything
